# Prompt Logic

## Goal

Generate SEO-optimized blog content and content clusters for a business website.

## Prompt Strategy

1. Defined the AI role as an SEO Content Strategist.
2. Defined the target audience.
3. Included local SEO keywords (Hyderabad).
4. Requested H1, H2, and H3 heading structure.
5. Focused on search intent and readability.
6. Included internal linking opportunities and content clusters.

## SEO Elements Used

* SEO-friendly title
* H1, H2, H3 structure
* Local SEO keywords
* Content cluster planning
* Internal linking strategy
* Search intent optimization
* Readable blog formatting

## Reason

Structured prompts help generate content that is optimized for search engines, improves user experience, and supports business visibility through SEO-focused content marketing.
